How We Work

A simple, honest process.

From the first message to the final delivery, here is what working with the desk looks like.

01 — Discuss

Send a message with what you need. We ask questions to understand the real problem, not just the surface request.

02 — Scope

We confirm exactly what you will get, when, and at what price. If it is not a good fit, we say so honestly.

03 — Deliver

We build it carefully and hand it over with clear notes explaining what was done and why each choice was made.

04 — Support

Have follow-up questions after delivery? Ask. Understanding is part of the work, not a paid extra.

Common Questions

Answers to the questions we hear most.

Not seeing what you need? Just send us a message and we will answer honestly.

How quickly do you respond to messages?

Within a few hours during working days, and always within 24 hours. We do not use auto-replies or scripts.

What if my project does not fit any of the four services?

Reach out anyway. Many projects blend across services or need something custom. If we can genuinely help, we will scope it clearly.

Do you offer revisions after delivery?

Yes. Every package includes revisions, with the number depending on the tier. Beyond that, small follow-ups are usually handled as part of the relationship.

Can you work on a tight deadline?

Often, yes. Send us the details and the timeline first. If we can genuinely deliver quality work in the time you have, we will confirm.

What if I do not understand something in the delivery?

Ask. Every delivery already comes with written notes explaining what was done and why. If any part is still unclear, we will happily explain further.

Do you take on ongoing or long-term projects?

Yes, for the right fit. Retainer-style relationships often work best for e-commerce growth and technical writing.
