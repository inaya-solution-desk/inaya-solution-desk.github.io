About the Desk

The story behind Inaya Solution Desk.

A quiet, focused practice built for the people who care about doing things right the first time and understanding them the second.

Where problems find a patient partner.
Est. 2026

Not another agency. A real desk.

Inaya Solution Desk was born out of a simple frustration. Too many freelancers hand over files and disappear. Too many agencies talk in jargon nobody understands. Too many learners are left staring at broken configurations, unfinished projects, or half-written reports with nobody to guide them through.

We built the desk to change that. Whether it is a Cisco topology that will not converge, an online store that needs to launch by next week, or a technical document that needs to speak clearly to real readers, we sit down beside you and figure it out together.

The name comes from the idea of a real, physical desk. A place where you can bring your problem, sit across from someone who has done this before, and walk away with an answer plus an understanding of how it works.

What We Stand For

Four values that shape every project.

These are not slogans on a wall. They are the standards we hold ourselves to on every message, every draft, and every configuration.

Clarity

Good work explains itself. If you cannot understand what we did or why, we have not finished the job yet.

Patience

Learning takes time. Ask questions, come back with more, and expect the same steady patience every time.

Craft

Every configuration, sentence, and design decision reflects a person behind it. We take pride in that.

Honesty

We tell you what is realistic, what is not, and what will actually help. Even when it costs us the sale.

The Difference

What makes the desk different.

The internet is full of people who will do a task for cheap. That is not what we do. We are for the people who want to actually understand what they are paying for and walk away smarter than they started.

Explanations included, always

Every deliverable comes with a short write-up of what was done and why. You never get a black box, you get a working solution and the story behind it.

Real conversations, not chatbots

Every message goes through a real human who reads it, thinks about it, and replies with something useful. No canned responses, no upsell scripts.

Scope confirmed before you pay

Before any order, we make sure we understand what you actually need and can genuinely deliver it. If it is not a good fit, we say so honestly and point you elsewhere.

Follow-up questions welcomed

After delivery, if something is unclear or a related question comes up, we answer it. Understanding is not a paid extra, it is part of the work.

The Journey

How the desk came together.

A short version of the road we have walked so far.

2021 — The Beginning

First freelance projects

Started with small Cisco Packet Tracer projects, quickly discovered that clients valued explanations as much as deliverables.

2022 — Finding the Style

Focus on teaching-through-work

Refined an approach that pairs every delivered project with clear notes explaining the reasoning. Repeat clients started coming back.

2024 — Broadening the Scope

Added technical writing and e-commerce

Expanded from pure networking into cybersecurity writing and small-business e-commerce projects, because the same principles apply.

2026 — The Desk Opens

Inaya Solution Desk is born

Formalized the practice under one brand: a focused, honest desk built for people who want real answers, not just delivered files.

Ready to Start

Have something you would like to solve together?

The desk is open. Whether it is a networking problem, an online store, a document that needs polishing, or just a question about what is possible, we would love to hear from you.
