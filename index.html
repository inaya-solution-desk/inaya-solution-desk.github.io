About the Desk

Your Trusted Networking Partner

Inaya Solution Desk is a focused practice built on one simple idea: every network problem has a clear solution, and every learner deserves to understand the reasoning behind the fix.

I am a network engineer with hands-on experience in Cisco Packet Tracer, GNS3, and Wireshark. I have helped everyone from confused learners to small business owners get their networks working. Not by handing them files and disappearing, but by walking through the problem, showing where things went wrong, and making sure they know how to fix it next time.

On the writing side, I produce clean cybersecurity and networking content grounded in real engineering experience. Because good documentation is what separates a network that works from a network that lasts.
