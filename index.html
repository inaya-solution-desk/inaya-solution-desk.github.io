About the Desk

A quiet place where hard
network problems get solved.

Inaya Solution Desk is built on one simple idea. Every network problem has a clear solution, and every learner deserves to understand the reasoning behind the fix.

I am a network engineer with hands-on experience in Cisco Packet Tracer, GNS3, and Wireshark. I work with everyone from confused learners to small business owners. Not by handing them files and disappearing, but by walking through the problem, showing where things went wrong, and making sure they know how to fix it next time.

On the writing side, I produce clean cybersecurity and networking content grounded in real engineering experience. Because good documentation is what separates a network that works from a network that lasts.
